<p><p>The control cable market encompasses various materials, primarily including PUR (Polyurethane), PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), and TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomers). PUR cables are known for their flexibility, durability, and resistance to abrasion and chemicals, making them ideal for demanding applications. PVC cables offer good insulation and a cost-effective solution for general applications. TPE cables combine the benefits of rubber and plastic, providing excellent flexibility, resilience, and environmental resistance. Each material caters to specific industry needs, influencing performance and application suitability.</p></p>
