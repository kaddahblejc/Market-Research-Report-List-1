Additionally, the trend toward larger vehicles, such as SUVs and crossovers, is influencing tire designs to cater to varied performance requirements.</p><p>Furthermore, the advent of smart tire technology, which integrates sensors for monitoring tire pressure and performance, is shaping future market trends.
