<p><p>The relationship counselling market encompasses various therapeutic approaches, including Cognitive-Behavioural, Emotionally Focused, and Solution-Focused therapies. Cognitive-Behavioural therapy targets negative thought patterns and behaviors, promoting healthier interactions. Emotionally Focused therapy emphasizes emotional bonding and attachment between partners, fostering deeper connections. Solution-Focused therapy shifts attention to finding practical solutions rather than delving into past problems, encouraging couples to envision and work towards their ideal relationships. Each approach offers unique strategies for couples seeking to improve or repair their relationships.</p></p>
