<p><p>The Ultrasonic Parking Guidance System market is segmented into two main types: Indoor and Outdoor Parking Guidance Systems. Indoor systems utilize ultrasonic sensors to detect vehicle occupancy, providing drivers with real-time information on available spaces within enclosed areas like malls or buildings. In contrast, Outdoor Parking Guidance Systems are employed in open-air parking lots, directing drivers to available spaces using similar technology but often integrating additional features such as digital signage and mobile app connectivity for enhanced user experience.</p></p>
