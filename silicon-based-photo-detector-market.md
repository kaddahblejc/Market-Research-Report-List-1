<p><p>The silicon-based photo detector market includes Silicon Drift Detectors (SDDs) and Silicon PhotoMultipliers (SiPMs). SDDs are known for their high energy resolution and low noise, making them ideal for X-ray and gamma-ray spectroscopy applications. SiPMs, on the other hand, are advanced devices that enable precise photon counting and are widely used in medical imaging, LIDAR, and high-energy physics. Both technologies offer advantages in sensitivity and performance, driving their adoption across various scientific and industrial sectors.</p></p>
