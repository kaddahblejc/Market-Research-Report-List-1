<p><p>The Organic Light-emitting Diode (OLED) materials market primarily consists of two types: PMOLED (Passive-Matrix OLED) and AMOLED (Active-Matrix OLED). PMOLED technology utilizes a simpler structure, controlling individual pixels through a direct connection, making it suitable for smaller displays and cost-effective applications. Conversely, AMOLED integrates an active matrix of transistors, enabling faster response times and higher resolution, ideal for larger screens and advanced devices. Both technologies play crucial roles in enhancing display quality and energy efficiency in modern electronics.</p></p>
