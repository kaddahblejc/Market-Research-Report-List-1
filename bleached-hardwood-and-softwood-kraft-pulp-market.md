<p><p>The bleached hardwood kraft pulp (BHKP) and bleached softwood kraft pulp (BSKP) markets focus on different types of wood sources for pulp production. BHKP is derived from hardwood trees like eucalyptus and aspen, characterized by a shorter fiber length, which enhances brightness and smoothness, making it ideal for high-quality paper products. In contrast, BSKP, sourced from softwood trees like pine and spruce, has longer fibers that provide strength and durability, making it suitable for packaging and high-strength papers. Both markets cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
