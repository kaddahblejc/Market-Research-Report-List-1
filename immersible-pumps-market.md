<p><ul><li>Non-clog Submersible Pump</li><li>Openwell Submersible Pump</li><li>Borewell Submersible Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immersible pumps market includes various types tailored for specific applications. Non-clog submersible pumps are designed to handle sewage and solid-laden liquids without blockage, making them ideal for wastewater management. Openwell submersible pumps are used in shallow water bodies, typically for irrigation and water supply, featuring a robust design suitable for variable water levels. Borewell submersible pumps are engineered for deep extraction of groundwater, commonly utilized in agriculture and residential sectors to supply water efficiently from deep subterranean sources.</p></p>
