<p><strong>The Light Sheet Microscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Light sheet microscopy encompasses various types, primarily including SPIM (Selective Plane Illumination Microscopy) and DiSPIM (Dual-View Selective Plane Illumination Microscopy). SPIM uses a single light sheet to illuminate a thin plane of the sample, allowing for rapid imaging with minimal phototoxicity. DiSPIM enhances this by using two light sheets for simultaneous imaging from different angles, improving resolution and contrast. Other market variations may include advancements in illumination sources, detector technologies, and integration with other imaging modalities, enhancing versatility in applications.</p></p>
