<p><strong>The EC Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Rotor Motor</li><li>Outer Rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EC motors, or electronically commutated motors, are categorized mainly into inner rotor and outer rotor types. Inner rotor motors have their rotor located inside the stator, offering compact designs and higher torque, making them suitable for applications like fans and small appliances. Outer rotor motors, conversely, feature the rotor on the outside, providing a larger surface area and better cooling, ideal for high-performance scenarios like electric vehicles and drones. Both types are recognized for energy efficiency and low noise operation.</p></p>
