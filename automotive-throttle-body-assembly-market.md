<p><strong>The Automotive Throttle Body Assembly Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Actuator</li><li>Throttle Plate</li><li>Throttle Position Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive throttle body assembly market is primarily categorized into three types: actuators, throttle plates, and throttle position sensors. Actuators control the airflow into the engine by regulating the throttle plate's position, ensuring optimal performance. Throttle plates are the physical components that open and close to manage air intake. Throttle position sensors monitor the angle of the throttle plate, sending data to the engine control unit for precise fuel-air mixture adjustments, enhancing efficiency and emissions control in modern vehicles.</p></p>
