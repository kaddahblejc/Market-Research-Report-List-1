<p><ul><li>Galvanic Oxygen Sensor</li><li>Catalytic Bead Sensor</li><li>Semiconducting Oxide Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solid-state gas sensor market comprises several key types, each serving distinct applications. Galvanic oxygen sensors measure oxygen levels using electrochemical reactions, ideal for medical and industrial uses. Catalytic bead sensors detect combustible gases through heat generation when gas oxidizes on a catalytic surface, widely utilized in safety monitoring. Semiconducting oxide sensors identify various gases by changes in electrical resistance caused by gas adsorption, commonly applied in indoor air quality and environmental monitoring. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing safety and efficiency across industries.</p></p>
