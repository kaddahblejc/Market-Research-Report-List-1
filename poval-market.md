<p><p>Poval market types are classified based on viscosity levels, which denote the thickness and flow characteristics of polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) solutions. Low viscosity (4.0~7.0) Poval is ideal for applications requiring easy manipulation, such as coatings and adhesives. Medium viscosity (21.0~33.0) offers a balance, suitable for various industrial applications like films and paper sizing. High viscosity (40.0~65.0) Poval provides superior thickening properties, making it effective in high-performance formulations, including cosmetics and specialty adhesives. Each type serves distinct needs across industries.</p></p>
