<p><p>The energy-saving lamps market includes two primary types: Self-ballast Fluorescent Lamps (electronic energy-saving lamps) and Single End Fluorescent Lamps (PL plug-in energy-saving tube lamps). Self-ballast fluorescent lamps integrate the ballast within the lamp, simplifying installation and improving efficiency, making them popular for residential use. Single-end fluorescent lamps are versatile plug-in options, offering efficient lighting for commercial spaces. Both types contribute to reduced energy consumption and lower environmental impact, making them essential in the transition toward sustainable lighting solutions.</p></p>
