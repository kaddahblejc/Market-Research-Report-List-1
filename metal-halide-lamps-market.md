<p><p>The metal halide lamp market is primarily divided into two types: quartz and ceramic. Quartz metal halide lamps use a transparent quartz envelope, offering high-intensity light and rapid warm-up times, making them suitable for applications like stadiums and industrial spaces. In contrast, ceramic metal halide lamps feature a more durable, opaque ceramic envelope, providing better color rendering and energy efficiency, making them ideal for retail and art galleries. Both types serve distinct lighting needs based on performance and application.</p></p>
