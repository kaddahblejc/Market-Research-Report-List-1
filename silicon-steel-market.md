<p><ul><li>Oriented Electrical Steel</li><li>Non-Grain Oriented Electrical Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon steel market consists mainly of two types: oriented electrical steel (OE) and non-grain oriented electrical steel (NGO). Oriented electrical steel is designed to enhance magnetic properties in one direction, making it ideal for transformers and high-efficiency motors. In contrast, non-grain oriented electrical steel has uniform magnetic properties in all directions, suitable for applications like electric generators and inductors. Both types play a crucial role in improving energy efficiency in electrical devices and supporting the transition to renewable energy sources.</p></p>
