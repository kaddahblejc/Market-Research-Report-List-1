<p><ul><li>Infrared</li><li>Heat Conduction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Carbon Dioxide Transmitters market is primarily categorized into two types: infrared and heat conduction sensors. Infrared transmitters operate by measuring the absorption of infrared light by CO2 molecules, offering high accuracy and sensitivity for real-time monitoring in various applications. Heat conduction sensors, on the other hand, detect changes in temperature caused by CO2 concentration variations, typically being more cost-effective but less precise. Both types serve critical roles in HVAC systems, environmental monitoring, and industrial applications by ensuring effective air quality management.</p></p>
