The market types include Three-bed RTOs, which utilize three thermal beds for efficient energy recovery, reducing operational costs and enhancing performance. Rotary RTOs feature a rotating media that continuously collects and releases heat, providing effective VOC destruction with a smaller footprint. Both types are essential for industries aiming for sustainable and compliant air quality solutions.</p></p>
