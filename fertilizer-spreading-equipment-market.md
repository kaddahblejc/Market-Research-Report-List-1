<p><p>Fertilizer spreading equipment is categorized into four main types: carriable, walk-behind, ride-on, and trailer. Carriable spreaders are lightweight and easy to transport for small gardens. Walk-behind models are manually pushed and suitable for medium-sized lawns. Ride-on spreaders are designed for large areas, allowing operators to comfortably distribute fertilizers while seated. Trailer-mounted spreaders attach to vehicles, enabling extensive coverage for agricultural fields and larger terrains. Each type caters to specific landscape sizes and user preferences, optimizing fertilizer application efficiency.</p></p>
