<p><strong>The Camera Microphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mono</li><li>Stereo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The camera microphone market is primarily divided into two types: mono and stereo microphones. Mono microphones capture sound on a single channel, making them ideal for situations where clarity of a single sound source is essential, such as interviews or recordings in controlled environments. Stereo microphones, on the other hand, utilize two channels to create a sense of space and depth in audio, making them suitable for capturing ambient sound and diverse audio landscapes during events, performances, or nature recordings.</p></p>
