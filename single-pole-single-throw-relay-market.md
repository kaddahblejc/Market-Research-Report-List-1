<p><p>The Single Pole Single Throw (SPST) relay market is primarily categorized into two types: Normally Open (NO) and Normally Closed (NC). Normally Open relays remain open until activated, allowing current to flow only when energized; this is commonly used in applications where a circuit should only complete upon demand. Conversely, Normally Closed relays are energized to open the circuit, maintaining a current flow until triggered; they are often utilized in safety systems to ensure operation halts under specific conditions.</p></p>
