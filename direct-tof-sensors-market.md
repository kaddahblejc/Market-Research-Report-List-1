<p><strong>The Direct ToF Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical ToF Sensors</li><li>Electromagnetic ToF Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Direct Time-of-Flight (ToF) sensors market encompasses Optical ToF sensors and Electromagnetic ToF sensors. Optical ToF sensors utilize light pulses to measure distances by calculating the time it takes for the light to return after reflecting off an object. In contrast, Electromagnetic ToF sensors use electromagnetic waves, such as microwave signals, to achieve similar distance measurements. Both sensor types are integral in applications like robotics, autonomous vehicles, and industrial automation, enabling precise spatial awareness and object detection.</p></p>
