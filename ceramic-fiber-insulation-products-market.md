<p><p>The ceramic fiber insulation products market includes various types, primarily ceramic fiber blankets and ceramic fiber boards. Ceramic fiber blankets are flexible, lightweight insulating materials used in high-temperature applications, ideal for industrial furnaces and kilns. They provide excellent thermal performance and resistance to heat transfer. Ceramic fiber boards, on the other hand, offer rigid insulation solutions, providing structural integrity and durability in high-temperature environments. Both types are valued for their ability to withstand extreme conditions while minimizing heat loss.</p></p>
