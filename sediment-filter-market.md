<p><strong>The Sediment Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Filters</li><li>Depth Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sediment filter market is primarily categorized into surface filters and depth filters. Surface filters trap particulates on the filter's surface, making them ideal for removing larger particles, sediment, and debris from liquids. They are typically easy to clean and replace. In contrast, depth filters allow contaminants to be captured throughout the filter media, providing greater filtration efficiency for finer particles and longer service life. These distinctions cater to diverse applications in industries like water purification, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
