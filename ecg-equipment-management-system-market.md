<p><p>The ECG Equipment & Management System market encompasses various types of devices and technologies used for heart monitoring and management. Holter monitors provide continuous heart rhythm recording over 24-48 hours. Resting ECG systems capture heart activity at a single point in time. Stress ECG monitors evaluate heart performance during physical exertion. Event monitoring systems record irregularities over extended periods. ECG management systems facilitate the storage and analysis of ECG data. Other devices may include portable and wearable monitors, enhancing patient care.</p></p>
