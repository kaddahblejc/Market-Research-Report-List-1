<p><p>Vacuum clamping systems are essential in various industries for securely holding workpieces during machining and manufacturing processes. The market is divided into two primary types: hose-based and hose-free systems. Hose-based systems utilize flexible hoses to connect the vacuum source to the clamping unit, offering versatility and ease of setup. In contrast, hose-free systems integrate the vacuum mechanism directly within the clamping surface, providing a more compact design and streamlined operation, often enhancing precision and efficiency in production processes.</p></p>
