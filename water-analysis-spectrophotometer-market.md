<p><strong>The Water Analysis Spectrophotometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Beam</li><li>Double Beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water analysis spectrophotometer market is segmented into single beam and double beam types. Single beam spectrophotometers utilize one light path to measure absorbance, which can lead to fluctuations due to light source variability. In contrast, double beam spectrophotometers employ two light paths: one for the sample and another for a reference, enabling more accurate and stable measurements by compensating for light source changes. This differentiation enhances precision in water quality assessments, catering to various analytical needs.</p></p>
