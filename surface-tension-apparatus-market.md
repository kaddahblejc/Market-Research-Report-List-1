<p><p>The Surface Tension Apparatus market is divided into two main types: Static Surface Tension Test Equipment and Dynamic Surface Tension Test Equipment. Static surface tension test equipment measures the surface tension of liquids under equilibrium conditions, often using methods like the drop weight or capillary rise technique. In contrast, dynamic surface tension test equipment assesses surface tension changes during processes like spreading or bubble formation, providing insights into the behavior of surfactants and foams in real-time applications.</p></p>
