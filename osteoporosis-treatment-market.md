<p><p>The osteoporosis treatment market encompasses several therapeutic categories. Bisphosphonates are the most widely prescribed, inhibiting bone resorption to increase bone density. Parathyroid hormone therapy stimulates bone formation, providing an anabolic approach to treatment. Calcitonin, a hormone, reduces bone loss and can relieve pain. Selective estrogen receptor modulators (SERMs) mimic estrogen's protective effects on bones while reducing the risk of breast cancer. Together, these therapies address various aspects of osteoporosis management, targeting prevention and treatment.</p></p>
