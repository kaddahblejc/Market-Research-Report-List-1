<p><p>The meteorological radars market is primarily divided into two types: airborne weather radars and terrestrial weather radars. Airborne weather radars are mounted on aircraft, allowing for real-time monitoring of atmospheric conditions and storm systems from an aerial perspective, essential for flight safety and weather prediction. Terrestrial weather radars, installed on the ground, provide continuous surveillance of weather patterns, detecting precipitation, wind speed, and severe weather events to inform forecasting and emergency management on a broader geographic scale.</p></p>
