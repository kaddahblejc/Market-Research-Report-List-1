<p><p>The plastic container market includes several key types: PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is widely used for its strength and recyclability, often found in beverage bottles. PP (Polypropylene) is valued for its versatility and resistance to chemicals, commonly used in food containers. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) offers durability and stiffness, ideal for milk jugs and detergent bottles. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is noted for its hardness and is used in construction materials, while LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is flexible and used for plastic bags and wraps.</p></p>
