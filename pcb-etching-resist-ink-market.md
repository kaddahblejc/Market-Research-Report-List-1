<p><p>PCB etching resist inks are critical for circuit board fabrication, protecting specific areas during the etching process. Photocuring inks harden when exposed to light, allowing for precise patterning and rapid production. UV curable inks offer similar benefits, curing instantly under ultraviolet light, enabling enhanced efficiency and flexibility. Thermal curable inks, on the other hand, require heat for curing, providing durable resist layers suitable for high-temperature applications. Together, these types cater to diverse industrial needs and production methods in PCB manufacturing.</p></p>
