<p><strong>The Disposable Manual Resuscitator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-inflating Resuscitator</li><li>Flow-inflating Resuscitator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Disposable Manual Resuscitator market includes two primary types: self-inflating resuscitators and flow-inflating resuscitators. Self-inflating resuscitators feature a bag that automatically refills when released, allowing for easy operation in emergency situations without the need for a supplementary gas source. In contrast, flow-inflating resuscitators require a constant gas flow from a source, providing controlled ventilation and enabling the caregiver to adjust pressure and volume more precisely. Both types are crucial for effective manual ventilation in medical emergencies.</p></p>
