<p><p>Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) equipment types include Cathodic Arc Vapor Deposition, Electron Beam Vapor Deposition, and Sputter Deposition. Cathodic Arc utilizes an electric arc to vaporize material, creating dense coatings. Electron Beam Vapor Deposition employs high-energy electrons to melt and vaporize the target material, offering precise control. Sputter Deposition involves bombarding a target with ions to eject atoms, facilitating thin films. Additional methods like thermal evaporation and others further diversify the PVD market, catering to various industrial applications.</p></p>
