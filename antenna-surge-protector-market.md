<p><p>The Antenna Surge Protector Market comprises three main types: Switch Type, Straight-through Type, and Composite Type. </p><p>Switch Type surge protectors offer manual operation to block or allow signal flow, providing control over the protection mechanism. Straight-through Type allows signals to pass through while actively protecting against surges, making it ideal for continuous performance. Composite Type combines elements of both, offering flexibility and protection in various applications. Each type caters to different needs in safeguarding antenna systems from voltage spikes.</p></p>
