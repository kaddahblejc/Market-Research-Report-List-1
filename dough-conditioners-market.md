<p><p>The dough conditioners market can be categorized into three main types: powder, liquid, and paste. Powder dough conditioners offer convenience and longer shelf life, making them popular for large-scale production. Liquid conditioners provide easy mixing and immediate incorporation into dough, enhancing consistency and performance. Paste conditioners are thicker, often used for specialty applications, and may contain specific enzymes for targeted effects in dough properties. Each type serves unique purposes in improving the quality and texture of baked goods.</p></p>
