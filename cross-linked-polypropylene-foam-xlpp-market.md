<p><p>Cross-linked Polypropylene Foam (XLPP) is an advanced material known for its durability and lightweight properties, making it suitable for various applications. The market is divided into two main types: physically cross-linked and chemically cross-linked polypropylene foam. </p><p>Physically cross-linked XLPP is produced through methods like heat and pressure, enhancing its robustness without additives. In contrast, chemically cross-linked XLPP involves polymerization techniques that create strong molecular bonds, offering superior thermal stability and resistance, making both types valuable in packaging, automotive, and construction industries.</p></p>
