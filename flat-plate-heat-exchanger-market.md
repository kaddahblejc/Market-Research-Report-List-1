<p><p>The flat plate heat exchanger market comprises three main types: gasketed, fully welded, and brazed heat exchangers. Gasketed heat exchangers utilize removable gaskets for easy maintenance and cleaning, suitable for varying applications. Fully welded heat exchangers offer robust construction for high-pressure and temperature environments, making them ideal for industrial processes. Brazed heat exchangers feature plates bonded together with brazing material, providing compactness and high thermal efficiency, often used in refrigeration and HVAC systems. Each type serves distinct operational needs.</p></p>
