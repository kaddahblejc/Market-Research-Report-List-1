<p><p>The drone detection radar market primarily consists of three types: FMCW (Frequency Modulated Continuous Wave) Radar, Pulse-Doppler Radar, and other specialized systems. FMCW Radar continuously transmits signals, allowing for precise distance measurement and target velocity, making it efficient for detecting various drone sizes. Pulse-Doppler Radar employs short pulses and measures frequency shifts, enabling it to differentiate between stationary and moving objects effectively. Other market types include advanced radar systems with integrated technologies for enhanced target identification and tracking capabilities, addressing diverse operational needs.</p></p>
