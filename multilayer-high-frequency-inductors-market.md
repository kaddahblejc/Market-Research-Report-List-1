<p><p>The Multilayer High Frequency Inductors market is primarily categorized into two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) Type and Plug-in Type. SMD inductors are designed for surface mounting, allowing for compact designs and efficient space utilization on printed circuit boards, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. In contrast, Plug-in inductors are used in traditional setups, featuring through-hole connections that provide robust mechanical stability. Both types cater to diverse applications in telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics, emphasizing performance and reliability.</p></p>
