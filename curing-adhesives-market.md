<p><p>The curing adhesives market comprises three primary types: UV cure, moisture cure, and thermal cure adhesives. UV cure adhesives utilize ultraviolet light to initiate a rapid curing process, ideal for applications requiring quick setting times. Moisture cure adhesives activate in the presence of moisture, making them suitable for outdoor or humid environments. Thermal cure adhesives rely on heat to activate their curing process, providing strong bonds in high-temperature applications. Each type caters to specific bonding requirements across various industries.</p></p>
