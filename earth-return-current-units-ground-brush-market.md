<p><p>Earth Return Current Units, or Ground Brushes, are essential components in electrical systems, particularly in railways and large industrial applications, to facilitate the safe return of current to the ground. The market for these units can be divided into axial and radial types. Axial ground brushes are designed for linear applications and efficiently direct current along a straight path. In contrast, radial ground brushes are structured to manage current in a circular or diverging direction, suitable for applications requiring a broader current distribution.</p></p>
