<p><p>In Vivo Imaging Systems encompass various modalities used for visualizing biological processes within living organisms. Digital Angiography specializes in vascular imaging, offering insights into blood flow and vessel abnormalities. Ultrasound uses sound waves for real-time imaging, widely used in prenatal care. Nuclear Imaging employs radioactive substances to visualize metabolic processes. Optical Imaging (OI) uses light for tissue analysis, focusing on cellular dynamics. The "Others" category includes emerging technologies and hybrid systems.</p></p>
