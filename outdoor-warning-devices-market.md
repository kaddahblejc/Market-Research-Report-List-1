<p><p>Outdoor Warning Devices (OWDs) can be categorized into three main market types: omni-directional, rotating, and directional. Omni-directional devices emit sound or visual signals in all directions, ensuring widespread coverage. Rotating devices feature a mechanism that rotates, projecting signals over a larger area and enhancing visibility or audibility. Directional devices focus their alerts in a specific direction, making them effective for targeted notifications. Each type serves different needs in emergency communication and public safety, tailored to specific environments and applications.</p></p>
