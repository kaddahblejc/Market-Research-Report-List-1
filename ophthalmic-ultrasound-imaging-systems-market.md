<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Ultrasound Imaging Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-Scan</li><li>B-Scan</li><li>Combined Scan</li><li>Pachymetry</li><li>Ultrasound Bio Microscope (UBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ophthalmic ultrasound imaging systems encompass several types, each serving unique diagnostic purposes. A-Scan provides axial length measurements of the eye, vital for cataract surgery planning. B-Scan offers two-dimensional images of the eye's internal structures, useful for detecting abnormalities. Combined Scan integrates A- and B-Scan functionalities for comprehensive assessments. Pachymetry measures corneal thickness, essential for glaucoma management. Ultrasound Bio Microscope (UBM) delivers high-resolutionimages of anterior segment structures, aiding in complex diagnoses and surgical planning. These tools enhance diagnostic accuracy and treatment efficacy in ophthalmology.</p></p>
