<p><ul><li>Wire-Wound RTD</li><li>Thin Film RTD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RTD (Resistance Temperature Detector) sensors market primarily features two types: wire-wound RTD and thin film RTD. Wire-wound RTDs use a coiled wire of pure metal, usually platinum, to provide high accuracy and stability, making them suitable for industrial applications. Thin film RTDs, on the other hand, use a thin layer of metal deposited on a ceramic substrate, offering a compact design and faster response times, ideal for applications requiring space efficiency and rapid thermal changes.</p></p>
