<p><p>The high-end hand dryer market primarily features two types: hot air dryers and jet air dryers. Hot air dryers use warm air to evaporate moisture from hands, providing a gentle drying experience but often taking longer to dry. In contrast, jet air dryers utilize powerful jets of air to rapidly wipe water off hands, ensuring quick and efficient drying. Both types emphasize energy efficiency and hygiene, making them popular choices for public restrooms and commercial facilities.</p></p>
