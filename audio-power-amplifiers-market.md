<p><p>The audio power amplifier market includes various types, each with distinct characteristics. Class-A amplifiers offer high fidelity but low efficiency, suitable for audiophiles. Class-B amplifiers provide better efficiency but can introduce distortion. Class-A/B combines benefits of both, balancing quality and efficiency. Class-G and Class-H enhance efficiency further by adjusting power supply voltage, minimizing heat generation. Class-D amplifiers use digital methods for high efficiency and compact size, making them popular in portable and high-power applications. Each type serves specific user needs.</p></p>
