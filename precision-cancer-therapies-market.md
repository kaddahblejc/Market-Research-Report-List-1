<p><p>The Precision Cancer Therapies market encompasses various innovative treatments tailored to individual patient profiles. Targeted therapy focuses on specific cancer cell characteristics, minimizing damage to healthy tissue. Monoclonal antibody therapy uses lab-engineered antibodies to target cancer cells, while gene therapy aims to correct genetic defects or introduce new genes to combat cancer. Together, these approaches enhance treatment efficacy and personalize cancer care.</p></p>
