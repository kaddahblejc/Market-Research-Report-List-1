<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Pumps</li><li>Piston/ Plunger Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metering pumps are precision devices used to deliver accurate fluid quantities in various industries. Diaphragm pumps utilize a flexible membrane to create suction and discharge, ensuring leakage-free operation, ideal for corrosive or viscous fluids. Piston or plunger pumps utilize a reciprocating mechanism for high-pressure applications, making them suitable for applications requiring precise flow rates. Both types are essential for chemical dosing, water treatment, and pharmaceutical processes, offering reliability and accuracy in fluid management.</p></p>
