<p><p>The Bale Netwrap market is segmented into two primary types: knitted net wrap and extruded net wrap. Knitted net wrap is created through interlocking yarns, offering flexibility and strength, making it suitable for various baling applications. In contrast, extruded net wrap is produced by melting and forming plastic into a continuous sheet, providing a durable and cost-effective solution. Both types serve the agricultural industry by ensuring bales are securely wrapped, preserving quality during storage and transportation.</p></p>
